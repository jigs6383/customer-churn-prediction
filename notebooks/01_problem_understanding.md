# 01 Problem Understanding

## Goal

Understand customer churn and define the machine learning problem.

## Notes

- Customer churn means a customer leaves the business.
- Churn prediction is a binary classification problem.
- The business wants to identify high-risk customers before they leave.
- Recall and precision matter more than accuracy because missed churn customers are expensive.
